Mesh refining procedure

In this section the procedure how the mesh is refined is explained in detail. Figure 186 shows the global picture. The refinemesh.c routine is a subroutine of routine frd.c, which is used for the general frd-output. At first the following routines are called (in this section “element” and “tet” are used as synonyms):
